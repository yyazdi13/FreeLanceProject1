About GumDrop

GumDrop is a frozen yogurt food truck located in Austin, TX. With a variety of flavors, toppings, and specials, it's our goal to help locals beat the heat with a cool frozen treat!

Flavors:
  • Banana
  • Bubble Gum
  • Cake Batter
  • Red Velvet
  • Pumpkin
  • Vanilla

Toppings:
  • Oreos
  • Cake pieces
  • Bubble gum flavored candy
  • Hot fudge
  • Walnuts
  • Confetti sprinkles
